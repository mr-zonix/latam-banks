🏛️ Ownership & Family Context

Grupo Financiero G&T Continental is controlled by the Novella-Torrebiarte families, among Guatemala's most established business families (also linked to Cementos Progreso). Key characteristics:

  • Corporate focus: Historically strong in corporate & commercial banking
  • Conservative approach: Stability and proven solutions valued
  • Long-term relationships: They value partners over vendors
  • Premium positioning: Not competing on price, competing on service
  • Family values: Traditional business ethics, relationship-based decisions

💡 Sales tip: G&T values stability and proven track records. Don't oversell innovation — sell reliability and long-term partnership.
