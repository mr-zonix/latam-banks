🎯 Strategic Opportunities — Paraguay Market

🥇 Core Banking Modernization

Continental and Atlas actively evaluating. COBIS contracts expiring. High appetite for cloud-native platforms.

🥈 Digital Banking Platforms

Local banks need to compete with Itaú's digital experience. Mobile-first is priority. Corporate digital lagging.

🥉 Agribusiness & Trade Finance

Paraguay = agricultural powerhouse. Banks need specialized platforms for commodity finance, trade docs, FX.

📋 Regulatory Context

  • Regulator: Banco Central del Paraguay (BCP) — bcp.gov.py
  • Banking Law: Ley 861/1996 de Bancos, Financieras y Otras Entidades
  • Capital Requirements: Basel III aligned, min 10% CAR
  • Currency: Guaraní (PYG), but ~40% of deposits in USD
  • Key Focus: Financial inclusion, digital payments (SIPAP), AML compliance
