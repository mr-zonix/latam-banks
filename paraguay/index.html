🎯 Strategic Opportunities — Paraguay Market

1

Core Banking Modernization

Several local banks on legacy systems. Continental and Sudameris actively evaluating modern cores. High appetite for cloud-ready platforms.

2

Digital & Mobile Banking

Rapid smartphone adoption (85%+) driving digital channel investment. Local banks need to compete with Itaú's digital platform.

3

Agribusiness & Trade Finance

Paraguay = agricultural powerhouse. Banks need specialized platforms for commodity finance, trade docs, FX management.

📋 Regulatory Context

  • Regulator: Banco Central del Paraguay (BCP) — bcp.gov.py
  • Banking Law: Ley 861/1996 de Bancos, Financieras y Otras Entidades
  • Capital Requirements: Basel III aligned, min 10% CAR
  • Currency: Guaraní (PYG), but ~40% of deposits in USD
  • Key Focus: Financial inclusion, digital payments (SIPAP), AML compliance
