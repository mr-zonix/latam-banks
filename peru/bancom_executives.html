Banco de Comercio • 🎖️ Military/Police Niche Bank • CPMP Owned

🎖️ CPMP Ownership — Key Context

BANCOM is 99.99% owned by Caja de Pensiones Militar Policial (CPMP), the pension fund for Peru's Armed Forces and National Police. The Board is appointed by CPMP, creating a quasi-institutional governance structure. Technology decisions may require CPMP board approval for significant investments. This is NOT a typical private bank — understand the military/institutional culture.

✅ Research Status

Key technology leadership confirmed. Board composition and CFO require additional verification. Small bank = lean leadership team with executives wearing multiple hats.
