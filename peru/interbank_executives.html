🏢 Intercorp Group — Local Powerhouse

Interbank is the financial arm of Intercorp, Peru's largest retail conglomerate (Supermercados Peruanos, Cineplanet, Inteligo, etc.). Unlike global banks, Interbank has FULL LOCAL decision authority. Known for digital innovation and modern approach — they compete aggressively with BCP.

⚠️ Research Status

CEO and Chairman verified from annual reports. Technology leadership roles require LinkedIn verification. Interbank is known for young, tech-savvy leadership team.
