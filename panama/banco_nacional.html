💡 Commercial Interpretation

Government bank dynamics: Lower ROE (8.2%) and higher CIR (58%) typical for state banks with social mandate. Efficiency opportunity: High cost structure indicates potential for digital transformation savings. Stable funding: Government deposits provide consistent, low-cost funding base. Modernization pressure: Political mandate to improve services and compete with private banks.

🔧 Technology Infrastructure — 6 Layers

Layer 1: Processing & Compute

Current State:
  • Primary DC: Government facility
  • DR: Secondary government DC
  • Legacy: IBM AS/400 (core)
  • HP ProLiant (distributed systems)
  • VMware (limited virtualization)
Cloud Strategy:
  • Cloud-reluctant (data sovereignty)
  • Private cloud study (2024)
  • Government cloud mandate pending
  • Hybrid approach being evaluated
Vendors:
  • IBM (legacy compute)
  • HP Enterprise
  • Local integrators (SONDA, etc.)
  • Public tender required
🎯 Opportunity: Major infrastructure modernization needed. AS/400 replacement is overdue. Private/hybrid cloud implementation. Public tender expected 2025.

Layer 2: Storage & Data Management

Primary Storage:
  • IBM Storwize (SAN)
  • HP 3PAR (secondary)
  • NAS: NetApp (file services)
  • Total: ~600TB
Backup & DR:
  • IBM Spectrum Protect
  • Tape backup (compliance)
  • Replication: SAN-based
  • RPO: 1 hour / RTO: 8 hours
Data Governance:
  • Manual classification
  • Government retention rules
  • No formal MDM
  • Data quality issues
🎯 Opportunity: Storage modernization required. Data governance implementation. Backup/DR improvement (RPO/RTO gaps).

Layer 3: Networking & Security Infrastructure

Network Architecture:
  • Cisco (legacy Catalyst)
  • Traditional MPLS WAN
  • No SD-WAN implemented
  • 72 branch connections
Security Stack:
  • Cisco ASA (firewalls - aging)
  • Symantec (endpoint)
  • IBM QRadar (SIEM)
  • No SOC — limited monitoring
Connectivity:
  • C&W + Claro (dual WAN)
  • SWIFT: AllianceGate
  • ACH local integration
  • Government network access
🎯 Opportunity: Major network refresh needed. SD-WAN for 72 branches. Security modernization (NGFW, SOC). High-priority compliance driver.
