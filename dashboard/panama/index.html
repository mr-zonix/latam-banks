RegTech & AML Solutions

Post-FATF greylist compliance is critical. All banks investing heavily in transaction monitoring, KYC, and regulatory reporting.

📋 Regulatory Context

  • Regulator: Superintendencia de Bancos de Panamá (SBP) — superbancos.gob.pa
  • Banking Law: Decreto Ley 9 de 1998 (modernized 2008)
  • Capital Requirements: Basel III compliant, min 8% CAR
  • FATF Status: Removed from grey list (2023) — ongoing monitoring
  • Key Focus: AML/CFT compliance, beneficial ownership transparency
