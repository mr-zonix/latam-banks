                    ┌─────────────────┐

                               │Infrastructure │

**Primary Path Options:**

1. **Efficiency Path:** COO → CIO → Infrastructure Director → CFO
   - Best for: Operational efficiency, automation projects
   
2. **Technology Path:** CIO → Infrastructure Director → CFO
   - Best for: Infrastructure refresh, modernization
   
3. **Business Path:** SME VP → CIO → Infrastructure Director
   - Best for: SME-focused digital tools

**Note:** Smaller organization = less bureaucracy, faster decisions, more accessible executives. But also = smaller budgets, need for right-sized solutions.

---

## Special Considerations

1. **SME Focus** — Technology should enable SME customer experience
2. **Efficiency Culture** — Pragmatic, ROI-driven decision making
3. **Public Company** — Investor relations and analyst coverage available
4. **Quito-Based** — Different business culture than Guayaquil banks
5. **Smaller IT Team** — May value managed services, simplicity
6. **Right-Sizing** — Don't propose Pichincha-scale solutions

---

## Competitive Positioning

Banco Internacional competes for SME customers against larger banks. Technology angle:

> "Help us serve SMEs better than Pichincha and Guayaquil can — with the agility and personalization a focused bank can deliver."

Position technology as competitive differentiator for SME segment, not just cost reduction.
